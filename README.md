# Sales Performance Analysis | SQL + Power BI
This project analyzes retail sales data using SQL and Power BI to identify performance gaps, profitability issues, and growth opportunities.


##  Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to uncover key business insights such as revenue trends, regional performance, and profitability.


## 🎯 Business Objective

The goal of this analysis is to evaluate sales performance, identify inefficiencies, and provide data-driven recommendations to improve revenue and profitability.



##  Business Problems & Insights

### 1. Uneven Regional Performance
- The North region generates the highest revenue, while the South and West lag behind.
- This indicates imbalance in market penetration and sales effectiveness.

**Recommendation:**
- Investigate sales strategies in high-performing regions and replicate them in weaker regions.
- Increase marketing efforts and promotions in underperforming regions.

---

### 2. Low Profitability in Some Product Categories
- Certain categories generate strong sales but relatively lower profit margins.
- This suggests high costs or excessive discounting.

**Recommendation:**
- Review pricing and discount strategies.
- Negotiate supplier costs or optimize sourcing for better margins.

---

### 3. Sales Dependency on Top Performers
- A few sales reps contribute a large portion of total revenue.
- This creates a risk if those individuals leave or underperform.

**Recommendation:**
- Provide training and performance support to lower-performing reps.
- Implement incentive structures to balance performance across the team.

---

### 4. Fluctuating Monthly Sales Trend
- Sales show noticeable fluctuations across months instead of steady growth.
- Indicates possible seasonality or inconsistent demand.

**Recommendation:**
- Identify peak and low seasons and plan inventory/marketing accordingly.
- Run targeted promotions during low-performing months.

---

### 5. Customer Behavior Differences
- Differences exist between new and returning customers.
- Retention may not be fully optimized.

**Recommendation:**
- Introduce loyalty programs and retention strategies.
- Personalize offers for returning customers to increase repeat purchases.
